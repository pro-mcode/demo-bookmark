Frequently asked questions

Got questions? Find quick answers about setup, features, and using the extension.

What is Bookmark

Bookmark is a lightweight browser extension designed to help you save, organize, and access your favorite websites with ease. Instead of cluttered tabs or forgotten links, Bookmark keeps everything in one place and syncs seamlessly across your devices, so your web is always within reach.

How can I request a new browser?

If your preferred browser isn’t supported yet, you can easily request it through our feedback form. We’re always working to expand compatibility, and your request helps us prioritize which browsers to add next.

Is there a mobile app?

Currently, our extension is designed for desktop browsers. However, we’re exploring mobile app options to make bookmarking even more accessible. Stay tuned for future updates!

What about other Chromum browsers

Yes! Our extension works on most Chromium-based browsers like Brave, Edge, and Opera. If you’re using a Chromium browser, you should be able to install and use it without issues.
